Administrative SMS Notifications

witless.org

Witless.org uses SMS only for administrative, operational, and account-related alerts. These messages are not marketing or promotional messages.

What these messages are for

Messages may include tool automation alerts, service health warnings, credential or login failure notices, infrastructure notifications, remote access or connectivity issues, email triage summaries, and similar system events.

Message types

Transactional and administrative alerts only. No advertising, telemarketing, or list sales.

Frequency

Message frequency varies based on system conditions, alerts, and automation events.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Subscribe to SMS Alerts

To opt in and receive administrative SMS alerts from Witless.org, send the following keyword via text message to our notification number:

Text START to +1 (512) 877-6613

After sending START, you will receive a confirmation message verifying your enrollment. Only users who text START to this number will be subscribed to alerts.
