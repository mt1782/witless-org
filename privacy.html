Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how witless.org collects, uses, and protects information related to its website and SMS notification workflows. witless.org uses SMS only for administrative, operational, and account-related alerts.

Information we collect

Depending on the service or workflow involved, witless.org may collect or process the following limited information:

  • Mobile phone number(s) explicitly authorized for SMS notifications
  • Message content and delivery records related to notifications
  • Basic technical logs, such as server, security, or service event logs
  • Website access logs generated by standard hosting infrastructure

How we use information

  • To send administrative, operational, security, and account-related text messages
  • To monitor system reliability, alerting, and troubleshooting
  • To maintain service integrity, security, and abuse prevention
  • To document consent, opt-out activity, and compliance where applicable

SMS privacy disclosures

Message frequency varies based on account activity, system conditions, alerts, and automation events. Message and data rates may apply.

Mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS consent, is not shared with third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing or promotional purposes. Full stop. No mobile opt-in data is sold, rented, traded, transferred, or disclosed for advertising, lead generation, or affiliate marketing.

How opt-in works

SMS recipients are added only through manual administrator configuration. There is no public website signup form for SMS alerts on this site. At present, SMS notifications are intended for the site owner’s own number. If additional recipients are added in the future, their number will be entered manually only after direct consent is obtained.

Opt-out

Recipients may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP. Recipients may request assistance by replying HELP or by contacting compliance@witless.org.

Cookies and analytics

This site is not intended to use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking, or third-party marketing analytics. Basic hosting or access logs may still be created automatically as part of normal web hosting operations.

Data retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operations, security, troubleshooting, compliance, or legal purposes.

Data security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@witless.org
Compliance or messaging questions: compliance@witless.org
